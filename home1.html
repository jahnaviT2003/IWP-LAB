Virat Kohli is an international cricketer who currently captains the India national team. He was ranked eighth on ESPN's list of world's most famous athletes in 2016. Born in Delhi, Kohli shot to fame as the Under-19 skipper, who led India to victory at the 2008 Junior World Cup in Malaysia. He got an opportunity in 2008 ODI as an opener in Srilanka when both Sachin Tendulkar and Virendra Sehwag were rested due to injury. He made his Test debut in 2011 and was part of the World Cup 2011 winning team.Virat Kohli is the only Indian batsman who got 890 rating points in the ICC ODI ranking. Previously Sachin Tendulkar got the best rating of 887 in 1998. Virat Kohli has the record of having the highest ICC rating points 922 in the ICC Player Rankings for Test Batsmen. Virat Kohli scored six double centuries as a captain in test matches. He surpassed Cricket legend Brian Lara, who had five double centuries as a test captain.
